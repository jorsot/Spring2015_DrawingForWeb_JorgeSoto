Added JavaScript to control the animations in the previous project. Clicking the Smoke button will toggle the eruptions of the volcano, and clicking the Time button will freeze the time of day, stopping the sky, stars, and ambient light animations.

The Turrialba volcano in Costa Rica has been temperamental lately, going through a series of violent eruptions covering large parts of the country in ash. For more info on the recent eruptions, take a look at this article.
